Features

  • Face detection and positioning
    • Detect faces in pictures and mark face coordinates to support simultaneous recognition of multiple faces
  • Face attribute analysis
    • Accurately identify multiple face attribute information, including age, gender, race, face value, expression, mood, face type, head posture, closed eyes, whether to wear glasses, face quality information and type, etc.
  • Emotional recognition
    • Analyze the sentiment of the detected face and return a confidence score that currently identifies 7 emotions such as anger, disgust, fear, happiness, sadness, surprise, and no emotion.

Application scenario

  • Smart member management
  • Smart campus management
  • Facial beauty
  • Interactive entertainment marketing
